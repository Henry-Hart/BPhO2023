This graph shows the elliptical orbits of planets in our solar system closer to the sun.

The graph is constructed by taking the 2d orbits of planets (which, with beta correction are still 2d planes) and aligning them. This is different from taking a 2d slice of all the orbits at once. Mathematically, this just means no beta correction.

This module is a flexible spirograph, with the ability to control time, position and parameters of planets.

When the graph is paused, the planets are draggable. Select planets using the menus. Custom A & B are user-defined planets. Time can be sped up / slowed down and the density of lines can also be controlled.
